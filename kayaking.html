About Kayaking Camping

Kayaking

My family likes to kayak when we can. Our kayaks are from Lifetime. We have a total of 8 kayaks. During the summertime we take our kayaks with us camping. We have not tried taking our dogs with us, which is what my dad wants to do. I think they like water too much to stay on. That would be an experience for sure!

Where I Kayak

I have kayaked in a couple of areas. The first time I kayaked was at Lake Cascade. I have kayed a small pond near Bruneau Dunes State Park. My family took our kayaks to the Oregon Coast in 2019. I kayaked in a bay, but it was to wavey for me!
